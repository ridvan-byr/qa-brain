# Priority Engine

Every finding receives a severity level.

---

Critical

Examples:

- Missing Assertions
- Authentication Issues
- Broken Isolation

---

High

Examples:

- Missing Boundary Tests
- Missing Negative Tests
- Missing Unicode Validation

---

Medium

Examples:

- XPath Usage
- Deep CSS Selectors
- Retry Misuse

---

Low

Examples:

- Minor Readability Issues
- Naming Suggestions

---

Priority Principles

Critical findings always appear first.

Higher business risk increases finding priority.
