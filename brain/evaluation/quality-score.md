# Quality Score

## Purpose

Quality Score measures how well a test is designed.

Coverage and Quality are independent metrics.

---

# Evaluation Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Readability | 15 |
| Maintainability | 15 |
| Assertions | 20 |
| Test Isolation | 15 |
| Stability | 15 |
| Data Quality | 10 |
| Best Practices | 10 |

Maximum Score = 100

---

# Quality Levels

90–100 → Excellent

80–89 → Good

65–79 → Acceptable

50–64 → Needs Improvement

Below 50 → Poor

---

# Principles

A large number of tests does not imply high quality.

Quality is determined by clarity, reliability and maintainability.
